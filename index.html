ABOUT

朱樹 音々は、写真を通して内面や感情を可視化する表現活動を行う写真家・研究者です

フォトセラピーを「自分で立つための道具」と捉え、作品・体験・研究を横断しながら、人が主体的に自分自身と向き合い、自立へと向かうきっかけを創出しています

国内外での展示や受賞歴を持ち、現在は Inner Vision Photography を軸に、展示・ワークショップ・研究を通して、その社会的価値の確立を目指しています

Nene Akagi is a photographer and researcher whose practice explores the visualization of inner states and emotions through photography.

She approaches photo therapy as a tool for standing on one’s own feet, creating opportunities for people to face themselves actively through artworks, experiences, and research.

With exhibition and award experience in Japan and abroad, she is currently developing Inner Vision Photography through exhibitions, workshops, and research.

PORTFOLIO

浄化 Purification

心に雨がそっと触れた瞬間、光の粒が静かに滲み渡り、私の思い込みを浄化する At the moment rain softly touches the heart, particles of light spread quietly and wash away fixed assumptions.

共存 Coexistence

異なるものがそこに存在するとき、世界は静かに新しい調和を見つけていく When differences are allowed to exist, the world quietly finds a new form of harmony.

再生 Rebirth

立ち止まった場所で、私は新たな道を見つけて進み出す At a place where I once stopped, I discover a new path and begin to move forward.

PROJECT

Inner Vision Photography は、言葉にできない内面の感覚や記憶、感情を写真として可視化するプロジェクトです

対話と撮影を通して、自分自身の内側と向き合い、気づきを得る体験を生み出します

フォトセラピーは、誰かに癒されるものではなく、自分で立つための道具です

Inner Vision Photography is a project that visualizes inner sensations, memories, and emotions that are difficult to express in words.

Through dialogue and photography, it creates an experience of facing one’s inner self and gaining awareness.

Photo therapy is not something that heals you from outside, but a tool for standing on your own.
